# AccessBridge AI PRD

## Product Summary

AccessBridge AI is an AI-powered opportunity readiness platform that helps small businesses find, qualify for, and apply to grants, contracts, supplier diversity programs, and business funding.

The product focuses on the execution gap: small businesses often find opportunities too late, misunderstand requirements, or lack the documents needed to apply. AccessBridge AI turns scattered opportunity access into a guided AI workflow.

## Hackathon Theme Fit

**Theme:** Transforming Enterprise Through AI

AccessBridge AI fits the theme by using AI agents and business intelligence to help small businesses become opportunity-ready while helping enterprise partners understand where entrepreneurs are getting stuck.

## Target Users

### Primary User

Underserved small business owners and early-stage entrepreneurs who want access to grants, contracts, supplier programs, and business funding.

### Enterprise User

Cities, nonprofits, workforce organizations, economic development agencies, and supplier diversity teams that want to improve opportunity distribution and track readiness blockers.

## Core Problem

Small businesses do not miss opportunities only because opportunities are unavailable. They miss them because the system is fragmented, confusing, and built for people who already know how to navigate it.

Common blockers:

- Opportunities are scattered across many portals
- Eligibility requirements are difficult to understand
- Owners do not know what documents they need
- Proposal and application language is intimidating
- Missing requirements are discovered too late
- Support organizations lack visibility into common readiness gaps

## Product Promise

AccessBridge AI does not just help entrepreneurs find opportunities. It helps them become ready to win them.

## MVP Goals

1. Let a user enter a business profile
2. Match the business to relevant mock opportunities
3. Show match score and readiness score
4. Identify missing requirements
5. Generate a capability statement
6. Generate a proposal draft
7. Generate an execution plan
8. Show an enterprise insight dashboard summarizing common opportunity blockers

## AI Agent Architecture

### Profile Analysis Agent

Extracts eligibility signals from the business profile.

### Opportunity Matching Agent

Matches the business against opportunity requirements.

### Readiness Scoring Agent

Calculates readiness based on documentation, insurance, experience, location, industry fit, and submission readiness.

### Application Builder Agent

Generates professional application materials.

### Enterprise Insight Agent

Aggregates blockers and turns them into actionable institutional insights.
